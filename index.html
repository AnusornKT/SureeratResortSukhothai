About us

Sureerat Resort Sukhothai is one of the most well-known resorts that located near Sukhothai Downtown. The location of the resort is about 12 kilometers away from Sukhothai Historical Park. At this place, we have make everything accessible and affordable for our guests, making they feel at home. We have been open this place almost a decade, and we're still willing to serve and provide all services our guests with happiness and kindness.
